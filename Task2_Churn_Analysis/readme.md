# Task 2 - Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed Telco Customer data to identify churn patterns,
key retention drivers, and customer lifetime trends
for a subscription-based business.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Google Colab

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle Telco Customer Churn Dataset
- Rows: 7,032
- Columns: 21

## Key Findings
- Total Customers: 7,032
- Churned Customers: 1,869
- Churn Rate: 26.58%
- Retention Rate: 73.42%
- Avg Monthly Charges: $64.80
- Avg Tenure: 32.4 months
- Highest Churn: Month-to-month contracts
- Senior Citizen Churn Rate: 41.7%
- Fiber Optic has highest churn rate

## Files
- `WA_Fn-UseC_-Telco-Customer-Churn.csv` - Raw Dataset
- `Untitled3.ipynb` - Analysis Notebook
- `Customer_Churn_Analysis_Report.pdf` - Final Report

## Insights
- Month-to-month contracts have highest churn rate
- Churned customers have avg tenure of only 18 months
- Churned customers pay higher monthly charges ($74.44)
- Senior citizens churn at 41.7% needs urgent attention
- Customers with longer tenure are more loyal

## Recommendations
- Offer discounts to convert month-to-month to annual contracts
- Introduce loyalty rewards after 12 months
- Create special retention plans for senior citizens
- Review Fiber optic pricing and service quality
- Proactively reach out to high charge customers
